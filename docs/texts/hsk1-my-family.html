HSK 1 · Newbie

我的家

Wǒ de jiā
My Family · 40 words · ~1 min
我wǒ家jiā有yǒu四sì口kǒu人rén。
There are four people in my family.
爸爸bàba是shì医生yīshēng,妈妈māma是shì老师lǎoshī。
My dad is a doctor, and my mom is a teacher.
我wǒ有yǒu一个yí ge妹妹mèimei,她tā今年jīnnián八bā岁suì。
I have a younger sister; she is eight years old this year.
我们wǒmen都dōu爱ài吃chī妈妈māma做zuò的de菜cài。
We all love the food my mom cooks.
我wǒ爱ài我wǒ的de家jiā。
I love my family.

Grammar note 语法点

口 — the measure word for family members

When counting people in a family, Chinese uses 口 instead of 个: 四口人. Asking about it: 你家有几口人?

我家有四口人。 — There are four people in my family.

Check yourself 小测验

1. How many people are in the family?
2. What is mom's job?
3. How old is the younger sister?
