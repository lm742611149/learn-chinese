HSK 1

我的家

Wǒ de jiā
My Family · ~1 min read
我wǒ家jiā有yǒu四sì口kǒu人rén。
There are four people in my family.
爸爸bàba是shì医生yīshēng,妈妈māma是shì老师lǎoshī。
My dad is a doctor, and my mom is a teacher.
我wǒ有yǒu一个yí ge妹妹mèimei,她tā今年jīnnián八bā岁suì。
I have a younger sister; she is eight years old this year.
我们wǒmen都dōu爱ài吃chī妈妈māma做zuò的de菜cài。
We all love the food my mom cooks.
我wǒ爱ài我wǒ的de家jiā。
I love my family.
