HSK 3 · Intermediate

地铁真方便

Dìtiě zhēn fāngbiàn
The Subway Is So Convenient · 109 words · ~2 min
以前yǐqián我wǒ坐zuò公共汽车gōnggòng qìchē上班shàngbān，路上lùshang车chē很hěn多duō，常常chángcháng迟到chídào。
I used to take the bus to work; the roads were full of cars and I was often late.
同事tóngshì问wèn我wǒ：“你nǐ为什么wèi shénme不bú坐zuò地铁dìtiě呢ne？”
A coworker asked me, "Why don't you take the subway?"
我wǒ试shì了le一次yí cì，发现fāxiàn地铁dìtiě又yòu快kuài又yòu便宜piányi。
I tried it once and found the subway is both fast and cheap.
从cóng家jiā到dào公司gōngsī只zhǐ要yào二十分钟èrshí fēnzhōng，比bǐ公共汽车gōnggòng qìchē快kuài多了duō le。
From home to the office takes only twenty minutes — much faster than the bus.
在zài地铁dìtiě上shang，我wǒ可以kěyǐ看书kànshū，也yě可以kěyǐ听tīng音乐yīnyuè。
On the subway I can read a book or listen to music.
地铁站dìtiězhàn里lǐ很hěn干净gānjìng，大家dàjiā都dōu排队páiduì上车shàngchē。
The subway station is very clean, and everyone lines up to board.
上个星期shàng ge xīngqī下xià大雨dàyǔ，路上lùshang的de汽车qìchē走zǒu得de特别tèbié慢màn，但是dànshì地铁dìtiě还是háishi很hěn快kuài。
Last week it rained hard and the cars on the road moved especially slowly, but the subway was still fast.
现在xiànzài我wǒ每天měi tiān坐zuò地铁dìtiě上班shàngbān，再也zài yě没有méiyǒu迟到chídào过guo，地铁dìtiě真zhēn方便fāngbiàn！
Now I take the subway to work every day and haven't been late once — the subway is so convenient!

Grammar note 语法点

为什么不……呢? — why not…?

A friendly way to make a suggestion: 你为什么不坐地铁呢?

你为什么不早点儿睡呢？ — Why don't you go to bed a bit earlier?
A 比 B + adjective (+ 多了)

比 compares two things; add 多了 for "much more": 地铁比公共汽车快多了。

今天比昨天冷多了。 — Today is much colder than yesterday.

Check yourself 小测验

1. How did the writer get to work before?
2. How long does the subway ride from home to the office take?
3. What happened when it rained hard last week?
