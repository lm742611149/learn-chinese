我的学校

Wǒ de xuéxiào
My School · 60 words · ~1 min
我wǒ是shì学生xuéshēng，我wǒ的de学校xuéxiào很hěn大dà。
I am a student, and my school is big.
学校xuéxiào里lǐ有yǒu很hěn多duō同学tóngxué和hé老师lǎoshī。
There are many classmates and teachers in the school.
我们wǒmen上午shàngwǔ学习xuéxí汉语Hànyǔ，下午xiàwǔ看书kànshū、写字xiězì。
In the morning we study Chinese, and in the afternoon we read and write characters.
我wǒ的de老师lǎoshī很hěn好hǎo，同学们tóngxuémen都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
My teacher is very nice, and the classmates are all happy.
学校xuéxiào前面qiánmiàn有yǒu一个yí ge小xiǎo商店shāngdiàn，我wǒ喜欢xǐhuan去qù买mǎi水shuǐ。
In front of the school there is a small shop, and I like to go there to buy water.
我wǒ很hěn爱ài我wǒ的de学校xuéxiào。
I love my school very much.

Key words 生词

Grammar note 语法点

Place + 里/前面 + 有 — there is / there are

To say something exists at a place, put the place word first, then 有: 学校里有很多同学。学校前面有一个商店。

学校前面有一个小商店。 — There is a small shop in front of the school.
都 before the verb

都 (all) goes after the subject and before the verb or adjective: 同学们都很高兴。

同学们都很高兴。 — The classmates are all happy.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What do the students do in the morning?
2. What is in front of the school?
3. What does the narrator like to buy at the shop?
