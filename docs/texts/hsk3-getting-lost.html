迷路

Mílù
Getting Lost · 113 words · ~2 min
上shàng个gè周末zhōumò,我wǒ一个人yí gè rén骑qí自行车zìxíngchē去qù公园gōngyuán玩儿wánr。
Last weekend, I rode my bike to the park alone to have some fun.
回家huí jiā的de时候shíhou,天tiān黑hēi了le,我wǒ发现fāxiàn自己zìjǐ迷路mílù了le。
On the way home, it got dark, and I found that I was lost.
手机shǒujī也yě没méi电diàn了le,不bù能néng看kàn地图dìtú,我wǒ非常fēicháng着急zháojí。
My phone was out of battery too, so I couldn't check the map, and I was extremely anxious.
我wǒ问wèn路lù边biān的de一位yí wèi老人lǎorén:“请问qǐngwèn,去qù中山路Zhōngshān Lù怎么zěnme走zǒu?”
I asked an elderly man by the road: "Excuse me, how do I get to Zhongshan Road?"
他tā热情rèqíng地de说shuō:“你nǐ一直yìzhí向xiàng前qián走zǒu,到dào了le红绿灯hónglǜdēng,再zài向xiàng右边yòubian走zǒu十shí分钟fēnzhōng就jiù到dào了le。”
He said warmly: "Keep walking straight ahead; when you reach the traffic light, walk to the right for ten minutes and you'll be there."
我wǒ很hěn快kuài就jiù找到zhǎodào了le回家huí jiā的de路lù,心里xīnli终于zhōngyú放心fàngxīn了le。
I soon found the way home, and my heart was finally at ease.
以后yǐhòu我wǒ每měi次cì出门chūmén,都dōu会huì先xiān看kàn好hǎo地图dìtú。
From now on, every time I go out, I will check the map carefully first.

Key words 生词

迷路mílùto get lost
地图dìtúmap
着急zháojíworried; anxious
一直yìzhístraight; all along
红绿灯hónglǜdēngtraffic light
终于zhōngyúfinally

Grammar note 语法点

一直 + verb

一直 means 'straight' with direction verbs, or 'continuously' with other verbs.

你一直向前走,到了红绿灯,再向右边走十分钟就到了。
adj + 地 + verb

地 turns an adjective into an adverb describing how an action is done.

他热情地说。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我周末骑什么去公园?
2. 我为什么不能看地图?
3. 老人让我到了红绿灯以后怎么走?
