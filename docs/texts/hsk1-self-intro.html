HSK 1 · Newbie

自我介绍

Zìwǒ jièshào
Introducing Myself · 38 words · ~1 min
你好nǐ hǎo!我wǒ叫jiào王小明Wáng Xiǎomíng。
Hello! My name is Wang Xiaoming.
我wǒ是shì学生xuésheng,今年jīnnián二十èrshí岁suì。
I am a student, twenty years old this year.
我wǒ家jiā有yǒu爸爸bàba、妈妈māma和hé我wǒ。
My family is my dad, my mom and me.
我wǒ喜欢xǐhuan看kàn书shū和hé看kàn电影diànyǐng。
I like reading books and watching movies.
认识rènshi你nǐ,我wǒ很hěn高兴gāoxìng!
Nice to meet you!

Grammar note 语法点

A 是 B — but never 是 + adjective

是 links two nouns: 我是学生 (I am a student). With adjectives use 很 instead: say 我很高兴, NOT 我是高兴.

他是老师,他很忙。 — He is a teacher; he is busy.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is his name?
2. How old is he?
3. What does he like?
