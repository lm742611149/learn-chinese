我的中国朋友

Wǒ de Zhōngguó péngyou
My Chinese Friend · 66 words · ~1 min
去年qùnián我wǒ认识rènshi了le一个yí ge中国Zhōngguó朋友péngyou,他tā叫jiào小龙Xiǎolóng。
Last year I made a Chinese friend named Xiaolong.
他tā对duì我wǒ特别tèbié好hǎo,常常chángcháng帮bāng我wǒ练习liànxí中文Zhōngwén。
He is especially kind to me and often helps me practice Chinese.
我们wǒmen一起yìqǐ吃饭chīfàn的时候de shíhou,他tā教jiāo我wǒ用yòng筷子kuàizi。
When we eat together, he teaches me how to use chopsticks.
一开始yì kāishǐ我wǒ用yòng得de很hěn不好bù hǎo,大家dàjiā都dōu笑xiào了le。
At first I was terrible at it, and everyone laughed.
现在xiànzài我wǒ不但búdàn会huì用yòng筷子kuàizi,还hái会huì做zuò几个jǐ ge中国Zhōngguó菜cài。
Now I can not only use chopsticks, but also cook a few Chinese dishes.
有yǒu朋友péngyou真zhēn好hǎo!
It's so good to have friends!

Grammar note 语法点

不但 A,还 B — not only… but also…

The second half must add MORE on top of the first: 不但会用筷子,还会做中国菜.

她不但会说汉语,还会写汉字。 — She can not only speak Chinese but also write characters.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is the Chinese friend's name?
2. What does he teach the writer to use?
3. What can the writer do now besides using chopsticks?
