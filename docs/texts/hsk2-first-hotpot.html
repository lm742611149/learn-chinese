第一次吃火锅

Dì-yī cì chī huǒguō
First Time Eating Hotpot · 98 words · ~2 min
昨天zuótiān晚上wǎnshang，朋友péngyou和hé我wǒ一起yìqǐ去qù吃chī火锅huǒguō，这zhè是shì我wǒ第一次dì-yī cì吃chī火锅huǒguō。
Last night my friend and I went out for hotpot — it was my first time eating it.
饭馆fànguǎn里li人rén很hěn多duō，非常fēicháng热rè。
The restaurant was crowded and very hot.
桌子zhuōzi上shang有yǒu羊肉yángròu、鱼yú和hé面条miàntiáo。
On the table there were lamb, fish, and noodles.
朋友péngyou告诉gàosu我wǒ：“肉ròu在zài锅guō里li很hěn快kuài就jiù好hǎo，别bié等děng太tài长cháng时间shíjiān。”
My friend told me, "The meat is ready quickly in the pot — don't wait too long."
我wǒ吃chī了le一块yí kuài羊肉yángròu，太tài好吃hǎochī了le，但是dànshì也yě很hěn辣là！
I ate a piece of lamb — it was so delicious, but also really spicy!
我wǒ喝hē了le很hěn多duō水shuǐ，眼睛yǎnjing都dōu红hóng了le。
I drank a lot of water, and my eyes even turned red.
我wǒ对duì朋友péngyou说shuō：“下xià次cì我wǒ还hái要yào来lái！”
I said to my friend, "Next time I want to come again!"

Key words 生词

Grammar note 语法点

第 + number — ordinal numbers

第 turns a number into an ordinal: 第一 (first), 第二 (second). 第一次 = the first time.

这是我第一次吃火锅。— This is my first time eating hotpot.
太…了 — so / too

太 + adjective + 了 expresses a strong degree: 太好吃了, 太贵了.

太好吃了！— It's so delicious!

Check yourself 小测验

1. What was special about last night's dinner?
2. What did the friend say about the meat?
3. What happened when the writer ate the spicy food?
