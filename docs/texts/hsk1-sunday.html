星期天

Xīngqītiān
Sunday · 45 words · ~1 min
今天jīntiān是shì星期天xīngqītiān，我wǒ不bù工作gōngzuò，我wǒ很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
Today is Sunday — I don't work, and I'm very happy.
上午shàngwǔ我wǒ在zài家jiā喝hē茶chá，看kàn书shū。
In the morning I stay home, drinking tea and reading.
中午zhōngwǔ我wǒ和hé朋友péngyou去qù饭馆fànguǎn吃饭chī fàn，菜cài很hěn好吃hǎochī。
At noon my friend and I go to a restaurant, and the food is delicious.
下午xiàwǔ我们wǒmen去qù看kàn电影diànyǐng。
In the afternoon we go to see a movie.
我们wǒmen都dōu爱ài星期天xīngqītiān！
We all love Sundays!

Grammar note 语法点

A 是 B

是 links two nouns to say A is B, like an equals sign: 今天是星期天 = today is Sunday.

今天是星期天。 — Today is Sunday.
很 + adjective

Chinese adjectives usually take 很 before them instead of 是: 我很高兴 = I am happy, not 我是高兴.

我很高兴。 — I am very happy.
A 和 B

和 joins two nouns, like English 'and': 我和朋友 = my friend and I. It does not join whole sentences.

中午我和朋友去饭馆吃饭。 — At noon my friend and I go to a restaurant to eat.

Check yourself 小测验

1. Why is the narrator happy today?
2. Where does the narrator eat lunch?
3. What do the narrator and the friend do in the afternoon?
