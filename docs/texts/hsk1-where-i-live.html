HSK 1 · Newbie

我住在哪儿

Wǒ zhù zài nǎr
Where Do I Live? · 52 words · ~1 min
你nǐ住zhù在zài哪儿nǎr？
Where do you live?
我wǒ住zhù在zài北京Běijīng，北京Běijīng很hěn大dà。
I live in Beijing — Beijing is very big.
我wǒ家jiā不bú大dà，我wǒ的de猫māo很hěn漂亮piàoliang。
My home is not big, but my cat is very pretty.
我wǒ爱ài去qù饭馆fànguǎn吃chī米饭mǐfàn和hé菜cài。
I love going to restaurants to eat rice and good food.
明天míngtiān我wǒ的de朋友péngyou来lái我wǒ家jiā，我wǒ很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
Tomorrow my friend is coming to my home, and I am very happy.
你nǐ想xiǎng来lái北京Běijīng吗ma？
Would you like to come to Beijing?

Key words 生词

住zhùto live (somewhere)
哪儿nǎrwhere
北京BěijīngBeijing
家jiāhome
饭馆fànguǎnrestaurant
高兴gāoxìnghappy

Grammar note 语法点

住在 + place

To say where someone lives, use 住在 followed directly by the place.

我住在北京。 — I live in Beijing.
哪儿 (where)

哪儿 asks about a place and sits where the place word would go in the answer.

你住在哪儿？ — Where do you live?
Statement + 吗

Add 吗 to the end of a statement to turn it into a yes-no question.

你想来北京吗？ — Would you like to come to Beijing?

Check yourself 小测验

1. Where does the speaker live?
2. Who is coming to the speaker's home tomorrow?
3. What is the speaker's home like?
