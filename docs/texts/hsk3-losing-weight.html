HSK 3 · Intermediate

减肥

Jiǎnféi
Losing Weight · 92 words · ~2 min
三月sānyuè的de一天yì tiān，我wǒ发现fāxiàn去年qùnián的de裤子kùzi穿不上chuān bu shàng了le。
One day in March, I discovered that last year's pants no longer fit.
妻子qīzi笑着xiàozhe说shuō：“你nǐ真的zhēn de胖pàng了le。”
My wife said with a smile: "You really have gotten fat."
我wǒ决定juédìng从cóng那天nà tiān开始kāishǐ减肥jiǎnféi。
I decided to start losing weight from that day on.
现在xiànzài我wǒ每天měi tiān早上zǎoshang六点liù diǎn去qù公园gōngyuán跑步pǎobù，也yě不bù吃chī蛋糕dàngāo和hé甜tián的de东西dōngxi了le。
Now I go running in the park at six every morning, and I no longer eat cake or sweet things.
一yì开始kāishǐ很hěn难nán，我wǒ常常chángcháng又yòu饿è又yòu累lèi。
At first it was very hard — I was often both hungry and tired.
但是dànshì三个月sān ge yuè以后yǐhòu，我wǒ瘦shòu了le五wǔ公斤gōngjīn，身体shēntǐ也yě越来越yuè lái yuè好hǎo。
But after three months, I had lost five kilograms, and my health kept getting better and better.
昨天zuótiān我wǒ又yòu穿上chuānshàng了le那条nà tiáo裤子kùzi，妻子qīzi说shuō我wǒ比bǐ以前yǐqián年轻niánqīng多了duō le！
Yesterday I put those pants on again, and my wife said I look much younger than before!

Grammar note 语法点

越来越 (more and more)

越来越 + adjective describes something increasing over time: 越来越好 (better and better), 越来越冷 (colder and colder).

我的身体越来越好。— My health is getting better and better.
比 … 多了 (much more … than)

A + 比 + B + adjective + 多了 makes a strong comparison: 我比以前年轻多了 (I look much younger than before).

今天比昨天热多了。— Today is much hotter than yesterday.

Check yourself 小测验

3. What happened after three months?
