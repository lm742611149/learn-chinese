HSK 1

我的狗

Wǒ de gǒu
My Dog · ~1 min read
我wǒ家jiā有yǒu一只yì zhī小狗xiǎo gǒu,它tā叫jiào豆豆Dòudou。
My family has a little dog called Doudou.
豆豆Dòudou两liǎng岁suì了le,很hěn爱ài吃chī。
Doudou is two years old and loves to eat.
每天měitiān早上zǎoshang,它tā和hé我wǒ一起yìqǐ跑步pǎobù。
Every morning it goes running with me.
我wǒ回家huí jiā的时候de shíhou,它tā很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
When I come home, it gets very happy.
我wǒ爱ài豆豆Dòudou,它tā是shì我wǒ的de好hǎo朋友péngyou。
I love Doudou — it is my good friend.
