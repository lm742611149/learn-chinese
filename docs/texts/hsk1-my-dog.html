HSK 1 · Newbie

我的狗

Wǒ de gǒu
My Dog · 47 words · ~1 min
我wǒ家jiā有yǒu一只yì zhī小狗xiǎo gǒu,它tā叫jiào豆豆Dòudou。
My family has a little dog called Doudou.
豆豆Dòudou两liǎng岁suì了le,很hěn爱ài吃chī。
Doudou is two years old and loves to eat.
每天měitiān早上zǎoshang,它tā和hé我wǒ一起yìqǐ跑步pǎobù。
Every morning it goes running with me.
我wǒ回家huí jiā的时候de shíhou,它tā很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
When I come home, it gets very happy.
我wǒ爱ài豆豆Dòudou,它tā是shì我wǒ的de好hǎo朋友péngyou。
I love Doudou — it is my good friend.

Key words 生词

只zhī(measure word for animals)
小狗xiǎo gǒupuppy; little dog
它tāit
每天měitiānevery day
回家huí jiāto come home
一起yìqǐtogether

Grammar note 语法点

…了 — a new situation

了 at the end of the sentence signals a change of state: 豆豆两岁了 = Doudou has turned two (it wasn't two before).

下雨了! — It's (started) raining!

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is the dog's name?
2. How old is the dog?
3. What do they do together every morning?
