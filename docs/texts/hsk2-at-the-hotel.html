在酒店

Zài jiǔdiàn
At the Hotel · 75 words · ~1 min
晚上wǎnshang八点bā diǎn,我们wǒmen到dào了le北京Běijīng的de酒店jiǔdiàn。
At eight in the evening, we arrived at our hotel in Beijing.
服务员fúwùyuán问wèn:“先生xiānsheng,您好nín hǎo,您nín叫jiào什么shénme名字míngzi?”
The attendant asked: "Hello, sir, what is your name?"
我wǒ告诉gàosu他tā我wǒ的de名字míngzi,他tā给gěi了le我们wǒmen一个yí gè大dà房间fángjiān。
I told him my name, and he gave us a big room.
房间fángjiān很hěn大dà也yě很hěn新xīn,我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn喜欢xǐhuan。
The room is big and new; we both like it a lot.
妻子qīzi说shuō她tā累lèi了le,想xiǎng休息xiūxi。
My wife said she was tired and wanted to rest.
我wǒ喝hē了le一杯yì bēi茶chá,看kàn了le看kàn手机shǒujī,就jiù睡觉shuìjiào了le。
I drank a cup of tea, glanced at my phone, and then went to sleep.

Grammar note 语法点

V 了 V (do briefly)

Repeating a verb with 了 in between means doing it briefly or casually.

看了看手机,就睡觉了。
adj + 了 (change of state)

了 after an adjective shows a new state has appeared: became tired.

她累了,想休息。
