我的老师

Wǒ de lǎoshī
My Teacher · 40 words · ~1 min
李老师Lǐ lǎoshī是shì我wǒ的de汉语Hànyǔ老师lǎoshī。
Teacher Li is my Chinese teacher.
她tā三十sānshí多duō岁suì,很hěn漂亮piàoliang。
She is in her thirties and very pretty.
她tā说shuō汉语Hànyǔ,也yě说shuō一点儿yìdiǎnr英语Yīngyǔ。
She speaks Chinese, and also a little English.
我们wǒmen不会bú huì写xiě汉字Hànzì,她tā教jiāo我们wǒmen写xiě。
When we can't write characters, she teaches us how.
我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn爱ài李老师Lǐ lǎoshī。
We all love Teacher Li.
