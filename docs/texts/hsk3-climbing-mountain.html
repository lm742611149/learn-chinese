爬山

Pá shān
Climbing a Mountain · 115 words · ~2 min
星期六xīngqīliù天气tiānqì很hěn好hǎo，我wǒ和hé三个sān ge同事tóngshì一起yìqǐ去qù爬山páshān。
The weather was great on Saturday, so three coworkers and I went mountain climbing.
早上zǎoshang八点bā diǎn，我们wǒmen在zài山shān下xià见面jiànmiàn，每个人měi ge rén都dōu带dài了le水shuǐ和hé面包miànbāo。
At eight in the morning we met at the foot of the mountain, and everyone brought water and bread.
一开始yì kāishǐ我wǒ爬pá得de很hěn快kuài，可是kěshì后来hòulái腿tuǐ越来越yuè lái yuè累lèi。
At first I climbed fast, but later my legs got more and more tired.
同事tóngshì对duì我wǒ说shuō：“别bié着急zháojí，我们wǒmen慢慢mànmàn爬pá。”
A coworker said to me, "Don't rush — let's climb slowly."
中午zhōngwǔ十二点shí'èr diǎn，我们wǒmen终于zhōngyú爬pá到dào了le山顶shāndǐng。
At twelve noon, we finally reached the top of the mountain.
从cóng山shān上shang往wǎng下xià看kàn，城市chéngshì里lǐ的de楼lóu都dōu变biàn得de很hěn小xiǎo，漂亮piàoliang极了jí le。
Looking down from the mountain, the buildings in the city looked tiny — it was incredibly beautiful.
我们wǒmen在zài山顶shāndǐng吃chī了le面包miànbāo，休息xiūxi了le半个bàn ge小时xiǎoshí。
We ate our bread at the top and rested for half an hour.
虽然suīrán回家huíjiā以后yǐhòu腿tuǐ很hěn疼téng，但是dànshì我wǒ还hái想xiǎng下个月xià ge yuè再zài爬pá一次yí cì。
Although my legs ached after I got home, I still want to climb again next month.

Key words 生词

爬山páshānto climb a mountain
着急zháojíto be anxious; to rush
终于zhōngyúfinally
山顶shāndǐngmountaintop
疼téngto ache; painful
极了jí leextremely

Grammar note 语法点

虽然 A，但是 B — although A, B

Unlike English, both halves appear in Chinese: 虽然腿很疼，但是我还想再爬一次。

虽然汉语很难，但是很有意思。 — Although Chinese is hard, it's very interesting.
Adjective + 极了 — extremely…

极了 after an adjective makes it very strong: 漂亮极了。

今天热极了。 — It's extremely hot today.

Check yourself 小测验

1. How many people went climbing in total?
2. What time did they reach the mountaintop?
3. How did the writer feel after getting home?
