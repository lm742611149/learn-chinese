HSK 1 · Newbie

你叫什么名字

Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi
What's Your Name · 53 words · ~1 min
我wǒ在zài学校xuéxiào认识rènshi了le一个yí gè同学tóngxué。
At school I got to know a classmate.
他tā问wèn我wǒ:“你nǐ叫jiào什么shénme名字míngzi?”
He asks me: "What's your name?"
我wǒ说shuō:“我wǒ叫jiào王小明Wáng Xiǎomíng,你nǐ呢ne?”
I say: "My name is Wang Xiaoming. And you?"
他tā说shuō:“我wǒ叫jiào李明Lǐ Míng。”
He says: "My name is Li Ming."
他tā二十èrshí岁suì,是shì中国人Zhōngguórén。
He is twenty years old and is Chinese.
我们wǒmen现在xiànzài是shì好hǎo朋友péngyou。
We are good friends now.

Key words 生词

认识rènshito get to know
同学tóngxuéclassmate
问wènto ask
名字míngziname
岁suìyears old
朋友péngyoufriend

Grammar note 语法点

你呢?

呢 bounces the same question back to the other person: 'and you?'

我叫王小明,你呢?
叫 + name

Use 叫 to give your name: 我叫 + name.

我叫李明。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我在哪儿认识他?
2. 他叫什么名字?
3. 他多少岁?
