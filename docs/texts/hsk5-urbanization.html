城市化

Chéngshìhuà
Urbanization · 150 words · ~2 min
最近zuìjìn几十jǐshí年nián,越来越yuèláiyuè多duō的de农村nóngcūn人rén离开líkāi家乡jiāxiāng,到dào大dà城市chéngshì去qù工作gōngzuò和hé生活shēnghuó。
In recent decades, more and more people from the countryside have left their hometowns to work and live in big cities.
这zhè种zhǒng现象xiànxiàng叫做jiàozuò“城市化chéngshìhuà”,它tā正在zhèngzài改变gǎibiàn我们wǒmen的de社会shèhuì。
This phenomenon is called "urbanization," and it is changing our society.
一方面yì fāngmiàn,城市chéngshì能néng提供tígōng更gèng多duō的de工作gōngzuò机会jīhuì和hé更gèng好hǎo的de教育jiàoyù条件tiáojiàn。
On the one hand, cities can offer more job opportunities and better educational conditions.
年轻人niánqīngrén在zài城市chéngshì里lǐ更gèng容易róngyì实现shíxiàn自己zìjǐ的de理想lǐxiǎng,收入shōurù也yě比bǐ农村nóngcūn高gāo得de多duō。
In the city, young people find it easier to realize their dreams, and incomes are much higher than in the countryside.
但是dànshì另一方面lìng yì fāngmiàn,城市化chéngshìhuà也yě带来dàilái了le不少bù shǎo问题wèntí,比如bǐrú交通jiāotōng拥挤yōngjǐ、房租fángzū太tài贵guì和hé环境huánjìng污染wūrǎn。
But on the other hand, urbanization has also brought quite a few problems, such as crowded traffic, expensive rent, and environmental pollution.
同时tóngshí,农村nóngcūn只zhǐ剩下shèngxià老人lǎorén和hé孩子háizi,很多hěn duō土地tǔdì没有méiyǒu人rén种zhòng。
Meanwhile, only the elderly and children remain in the villages, and much of the land has no one to farm it.
有人yǒurén认为rènwéi应该yīnggāi继续jìxù发展fāzhǎn大dà城市chéngshì,也yě有人yǒurén主张zhǔzhāng把bǎ更gèng多duō的de资源zīyuán放到fàngdào农村nóngcūn去qù。
Some people think we should keep developing big cities, while others advocate putting more resources into the countryside.
在zài我wǒ看来kànlái,城市chéngshì和hé农村nóngcūn都dōu需要xūyào发展fāzhǎn,关键guānjiàn是shì让ràng人们rénmen自由zìyóu地de选择xuǎnzé自己zìjǐ的de生活shēnghuó方式fāngshì。
In my view, both cities and the countryside need to develop; the key is to let people freely choose their own way of life.

Grammar note 语法点

一方面……另一方面……

'On the one hand... on the other hand' — presents two contrasting sides of an issue, very common in argumentative writing.

一方面,城市能提供更多的工作机会和更好的教育条件。
在我看来

'In my view / as I see it' — introduces the speaker's personal opinion, usually at the start of a concluding sentence.

在我看来,城市和农村都需要发展。
A 比 B + adj + 得多

'A is much more ... than B' — adding 得多 after the adjective intensifies the comparison.

收入也比农村高得多。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 越来越多的农村人到大城市去做什么?
2. 下面哪个是城市化带来的问题?
3. 作者认为关键是什么?
