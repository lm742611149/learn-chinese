HSK 1

在饭馆

Zài fànguǎn
At the Restaurant · ~1 min read
中午zhōngwǔ我们wǒmen去qù饭馆fànguǎn吃饭chīfàn。
At noon we go to a restaurant to eat.
我wǒ要yào一个yí ge米饭mǐfàn和hé一些yìxiē菜cài。
I order a bowl of rice and some dishes.
朋友péngyou喝hē水shuǐ,我wǒ喝hē茶chá。
My friend drinks water; I drink tea.
菜cài很hěn好吃hǎochī,我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
The food is delicious, and we are all very happy.
饭馆fànguǎn的de人rén说shuō:“欢迎huānyíng你们nǐmen再zài来lái!”
The restaurant staff says: "Welcome back again!"
