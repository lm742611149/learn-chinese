在饭馆

Zài fànguǎn
At the Restaurant · 43 words · ~1 min
中午zhōngwǔ我们wǒmen去qù饭馆fànguǎn吃饭chīfàn。
At noon we go to a restaurant to eat.
我wǒ要yào一个yí ge米饭mǐfàn和hé一些yìxiē菜cài。
I order a bowl of rice and some dishes.
朋友péngyou喝hē水shuǐ,我wǒ喝hē茶chá。
My friend drinks water; I drink tea.
菜cài很hěn好吃hǎochī,我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
The food is delicious, and we are all very happy.
饭馆fànguǎn的de人rén说shuō:“欢迎huānyíng你们nǐmen再zài来lái!”
The restaurant staff says: "Welcome back again!"

Key words 生词

饭馆fànguǎnrestaurant
米饭mǐfànrice
好吃hǎochītasty (for food)
高兴gāoxìnghappy
欢迎huānyíngto welcome
再zàiagain

Grammar note 语法点

A 的 B — possession with 的

的 links an owner to a thing: 饭馆的人 = the restaurant's staff, 妈妈的菜 = mom's cooking.

这是我的杯子。 — This is my cup.

Check yourself 小测验

1. When do they go to the restaurant?
2. What does the writer drink?
3. What does the restaurant staff say?
