去商店

Qù shāngdiàn
Going to the Shop · 50 words · ~1 min
下午xiàwǔ我wǒ和hé妈妈māma去qù商店shāngdiàn。
In the afternoon I go to the shop with Mom.
商店shāngdiàn里lǐ东西dōngxi很hěn多duō。
There are a lot of things in the shop.
我们wǒmen买mǎi了le六个liù gè苹果píngguǒ和hé一些yìxiē水shuǐ。
We buy six apples and some water.
苹果píngguǒ十shí块kuài钱qián,水shuǐ三sān块kuài钱qián。
The apples are ten kuai, and the water is three kuai.
妈妈māma说shuō:“这些zhèxiē苹果píngguǒ很hěn好hǎo。”
Mom says: "These apples are very good."
我wǒ很hěn喜欢xǐhuan和hé妈妈māma去qù商店shāngdiàn。
I really like going to the shop with Mom.

Key words 生词

商店shāngdiànshop; store
东西dōngxithings
买mǎito buy
苹果píngguǒapple
一些yìxiēsome
喜欢xǐhuanto like

Grammar note 语法点

A 和 B + verb

和 links two people doing something together: 'A does something with B'.

下午我和妈妈去商店。
verb + 了

了 right after a verb marks the action as completed.

我们买了六个苹果和一些水。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我和谁去商店?
2. 我们买了几个苹果?
3. 苹果多少钱?
