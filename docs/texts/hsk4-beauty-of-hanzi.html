HSK 4 · Upper Int.

汉字的美

Hànzì de měi
The Beauty of Chinese Characters · 195 words · ~3 min
很多hěn duō学生xuésheng刚gāng开始kāishǐ学xué汉字Hànzì的de时候shíhou都dōu觉得juéde难nán，甚至shènzhì想xiǎng放弃fàngqì。
Many students find Chinese characters hard when they first start, and some even want to give up.
可是kěshì我wǒ是shì中文Zhōngwén老师lǎoshī，我wǒ想xiǎng告诉gàosu你nǐ：每měi个ge汉字Hànzì的de后面hòumiàn，都dōu有yǒu一yí个ge小xiǎo故事gùshi。
But as a Chinese teacher, I want to tell you: behind every character there is a little story.
最zuì早zǎo的de汉字Hànzì很hěn像xiàng图画túhuà：“山shān”看起来kàn qǐlai像xiàng三sān座zuò山shān，“雨yǔ”里li的de四sì个ge点diǎn，就jiù像xiàng正在zhèngzài掉diào下来xiàlai的de雨yǔ。
The earliest characters were much like pictures: 山 looks like three mountain peaks, and the four dots inside 雨 look like falling rain.
有些yǒuxiē字zì放fàng在zài一起yìqǐ，意思yìsi就jiù更gèng有意思yǒu yìsi了le：一yí个ge“人rén”靠kào在zài“木mù”旁边pángbiān，就jiù是shì“休xiū”，休息xiūxi的de休xiū。
When characters combine, the meanings get even more interesting: a person (人) leaning against a tree (木) makes 休 — the xiu of 休息, "to rest".
汉字Hànzì不仅bùjǐn有用yǒuyòng，而且érqiě可以kěyǐ变成biànchéng艺术yìshù，中国人Zhōngguórén把bǎ写字xiězì的de艺术yìshù叫做jiàozuò“书法shūfǎ”。
Characters are not only useful, they can also become art — Chinese people call the art of writing characters "shufa", calligraphy.
学xué汉字Hànzì没有méiyǒu什么shénme秘密mìmì，你nǐ写xiě得de越yuè多duō，就jiù记jì得de越yuè清楚qīngchu。
There is no secret to learning characters: the more you write, the more clearly you remember them.
即使jíshǐ你nǐ每天měitiān只zhǐ学xué三sān五wǔ个ge字zì，一yì年nián以后yǐhòu，你nǐ也yě会huì认识rènshi一千yìqiān多duō个ge汉字Hànzì。
Even if you learn only three to five characters a day, a year later you will still know more than a thousand.
所以suǒyǐ别bié着急zháojí，有yǒu一yì天tiān你nǐ会huì发现fāxiàn，你nǐ不bú是shì在zài记jì字zì，而是érshì在zài认识rènshi一yì种zhǒng古老gǔlǎo的de文化wénhuà。
So don't worry — one day you'll find that you are not memorizing characters, but getting to know an ancient culture.

Grammar note 语法点

越……越……

"The more ... the more ..." links two things that grow together: 你写得越多，就记得越清楚。

雨越下越大了。 — The rain is getting heavier and heavier.
即使……也……

"Even if ... still ..." — the result holds even under an extreme condition: 即使你每天只学三五个字，一年以后你也会认识一千多个汉字。

即使明天下雨，我们也要去爬山。 — Even if it rains tomorrow, we will still go hiking.
不是……而是……

"Not A, but B" replaces a wrong idea with the right one: 你不是在记字，而是在认识一种古老的文化。

学好中文不是靠运气，而是靠练习。 — Mastering Chinese depends not on luck, but on practice.

Check yourself 小测验

1. According to the text, what does the character 休 (to rest) show?
2. What is "shufa"?
3. What does the author say about learning three to five characters a day?
