HSK 3 · Intermediate

请客吃饭

Qǐngkè chīfàn
Treating Friends to Dinner · 118 words · ~2 min
上shàng个gè月yuè我wǒ找到zhǎodào了le新xīn工作gōngzuò,所以suǒyǐ这个zhège周六zhōuliù我wǒ请qǐng朋友们péngyoumen吃饭chīfàn。
Last month I found a new job, so this Saturday I treated my friends to dinner.
我们wǒmen去qù了le公司gōngsī附近fùjìn的de一家yì jiā饭馆fànguǎn,那里nàlǐ的de菜cài又yòu好吃hǎochī又yòu便宜piányi。
We went to a restaurant near my company; the food there is both tasty and cheap.
我wǒ先xiān看kàn菜单càidān,给gěi大家dàjiā点diǎn了le六liù个gè菜cài和hé一些yìxiē啤酒píjiǔ。
I looked at the menu first and ordered six dishes and some beer for everyone.
我wǒ的de朋友péngyou小李Xiǎo Lǐ不bù喝hē啤酒píjiǔ,只zhǐ要yào了le一杯yì bēi甜甜的tiántián de果汁guǒzhī。
My friend Xiao Li doesn't drink beer; he only asked for a glass of sweet fruit juice.
我们wǒmen一边yìbiān吃饭chīfàn,一边yìbiān聊天儿liáotiānr,大家dàjiā都dōu非常fēicháng高兴gāoxìng。
We chatted while eating, and everyone was very happy.
吃chī完wán饭fàn,朋友们péngyoumen要yào给gěi钱qián,我wǒ笑xiào着zhe说shuō:“今天jīntiān我wǒ请客qǐngkè,谁shéi的de钱qián都dōu不bù要yào!”
After the meal, my friends wanted to pay, but I said with a smile: "Today it's my treat — I won't take anyone's money!"
他们tāmen说shuō下xià次cì他们tāmen请qǐng我wǒ,我们wǒmen就jiù这样zhèyàng决定juédìng了le。
They said next time they would treat me, and that's what we decided.

Key words 生词

请客qǐngkèto treat (pay for others)
菜单càidānmenu
点diǎnto order (food)
啤酒píjiǔbeer
聊天儿liáotiānrto chat
决定juédìngto decide

Grammar note 语法点

又……又……

'Both ... and ...' — links two qualities of the same thing.

那里的菜又好吃又便宜。
一边……一边……

'While doing A, also doing B' — two actions happening at the same time.

我们一边吃饭,一边聊天儿。
谁……都 + 不/没

A question word + 都 + negative means 'no one / nothing at all'.

谁的钱都不要!
