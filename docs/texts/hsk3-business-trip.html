HSK 3 · Intermediate

出差

Chūchāi
A Business Trip · 125 words · ~2 min
下xià星期一xīngqīyī,我wǒ要yào和hé经理jīnglǐ一起yìqǐ去qù上海Shànghǎi出差chūchāi。
Next Monday, I'm going to Shanghai on a business trip with my manager.
这zhè是shì我wǒ第一次dì-yī cì出差chūchāi,心里xīnli又yòu高兴gāoxìng又yòu有点儿yǒudiǎnr担心dānxīn。
It's my first business trip, so I feel both excited and a little worried.
星期天xīngqītiān晚上wǎnshang,我wǒ把bǎ衣服yīfu和hé电脑diànnǎo都dōu放fàng进jìn了le行李箱xínglixiāng。
On Sunday evening, I put my clothes and my computer into the suitcase.
第二天dì-èr tiān早上zǎoshang六liù点diǎn,我们wǒmen就jiù坐zuò出租车chūzūchē去qù机场jīchǎng了le。
At six o'clock the next morning, we took a taxi to the airport.
飞机fēijī上shang,经理jīnglǐ告诉gàosu我wǒ:“到dào了le上海Shànghǎi,我们wǒmen先xiān去qù宾馆bīnguǎn,再zài去qù见jiàn客人kèrén。”
On the plane, the manager told me: "When we arrive in Shanghai, we'll go to the hotel first, then go meet the clients."
下午xiàwǔ我们wǒmen开kāi了le三sān个gè小时xiǎoshí的de会huì,客人kèrén对duì我们wǒmen非常fēicháng满意mǎnyì。
In the afternoon we had a three-hour meeting, and the clients were very satisfied with us.
晚上wǎnshang,经理jīnglǐ请qǐng我wǒ吃chī了le上海Shànghǎi最zuì有名yǒumíng的de菜cài。
In the evening, the manager treated me to Shanghai's most famous dishes.
这zhè次cì出差chūchāi很hěn快kuài就jiù结束jiéshù了le,我wǒ觉得juéde自己zìjǐ学到xuédào了le很hěn多duō,真zhēn不bú错cuò!
The business trip ended quickly; I feel I learned a lot — it was really great!

Key words 生词

出差chūchāito go on a business trip
经理jīnglǐmanager
担心dānxīnto worry
行李箱xínglixiāngsuitcase
满意mǎnyìsatisfied
结束jiéshùto end; to finish

Grammar note 语法点

把 + object + verb + result

The 把 structure moves the object before the verb to show what is done to it, with a result after the verb.

我把衣服和电脑都放进了行李箱。
先……再……

'First ..., then ...' — states the order of two planned actions.

我们先去宾馆,再去见客人。
对……满意

'Satisfied with ...' — 对 introduces the target of the feeling.

客人对我们非常满意。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我和谁一起去上海出差?
2. 到了上海,他们先去哪儿?
3. 下午的会开了多长时间?
