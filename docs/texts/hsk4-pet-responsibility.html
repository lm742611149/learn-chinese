养宠物的责任

Yǎng chǒngwù de zérèn
The Responsibility of Having a Pet · 181 words · ~3 min
去年qùnián我wǒ在zài朋友péngyou家jiā看到kàndào一只yì zhī小狗xiǎogǒu，它tā又yòu可爱kě'ài又yòu聪明cōngming，我wǒ马上mǎshàng决定juédìng自己zìjǐ也yě要yào养yǎng一只yì zhī。
Last year I saw a puppy at a friend's house; it was so cute and clever that I decided on the spot I wanted one too.
可是kěshì把bǎ小狗xiǎogǒu带回dàihuí家jiā以后yǐhòu，我wǒ才cái发现fāxiàn养yǎng宠物chǒngwù没有méiyǒu想xiǎng的de那么nàme简单jiǎndān。
But only after bringing the puppy home did I discover that keeping a pet is not as simple as I had imagined.
每天měitiān早上zǎoshang六点liù diǎn，它tā就jiù把bǎ我wǒ叫醒jiàoxǐng，不管bùguǎn刮风guāfēng下雨xiàyǔ，我wǒ都dōu得děi带dài它tā出去chūqù散步sànbù。
Every morning at six it wakes me up, and no matter whether it is windy or raining, I have to take it out for a walk.
它tā生病shēngbìng的de时候shíhou，我wǒ要yào带dài它tā去qù看kàn医生yīshēng，有yǒu一次yí cì就jiù花huā了le一千yìqiān多duō块kuài。
When it gets sick I have to take it to the vet — one visit once cost more than a thousand yuan.
想xiǎng出去chūqù旅行lǚxíng的de时候shíhou，我wǒ也yě得děi先xiān找到zhǎodào愿意yuànyì照顾zhàogù它tā的de朋友péngyou，否则fǒuzé就jiù只能zhǐ néng放弃fàngqì计划jìhuà。
When I want to go traveling, I first have to find a friend willing to look after it; otherwise I can only give up the plan.
有yǒu一段yí duàn时间shíjiān我wǒ真的zhēnde很hěn累lèi，甚至shènzhì有点儿yǒudiǎnr后悔hòuhuǐ养yǎng了le它tā。
For a while I was genuinely exhausted, and I even slightly regretted getting it.
但是dànshì每天měitiān回家huí jiā，它tā都dōu会huì高兴gāoxìng地de跑过来pǎo guòlai欢迎huānyíng我wǒ，那时候nà shíhou所有suǒyǒu的de辛苦xīnkǔ都dōu变得biànde值得zhíde了le。
But every day when I come home, it runs over happily to greet me, and in that moment all the hard work becomes worth it.
现在xiànzài我wǒ常cháng对duì想xiǎng养yǎng宠物chǒngwù的de朋友péngyou说shuō：“宠物chǒngwù不是bú shì玩具wánjù，而是érshì一条yì tiáo生命shēngmìng，只有zhǐyǒu你nǐ能néng对duì它tā负责fùzé，才cái应该yīnggāi把bǎ它tā带回dàihuí家jiā。”
Now I often tell friends who want a pet: a pet is not a toy but a life, and only if you can take responsibility for it should you bring one home.

Grammar note 语法点

不管……都……

"No matter A, B still holds." 不管 introduces a condition that makes no difference, and 都 stresses that the result never changes, as in 不管刮风下雨，我都得带它出去散步.

不管刮风下雨，我都得带它出去散步。 — No matter whether it's windy or raining, I have to take it out for a walk.
甚至

甚至 means "even" and introduces the most extreme or surprising case in a list or statement, as in 我很累，甚至有点儿后悔养了它.

有一段时间我真的很累，甚至有点儿后悔养了它。 — For a while I was really exhausted; I even slightly regretted getting it.
只有……才……

"Only if A can B happen." 只有 states the one necessary condition, and 才 marks the result that follows only from it, as in 只有你能对它负责，才应该把它带回家.

只有你能对它负责，才应该把它带回家。 — Only if you can take responsibility for it should you bring it home.
