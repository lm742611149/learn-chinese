人工智能与工作

Réngōng zhìnéng yǔ gōngzuò
AI and Work · 178 words · ~3 min
最近zuìjìn，我们wǒmen公司gōngsī开始kāishǐ用yòng人工智能réngōng zhìnéng写xiě报告bàogào，同事们tóngshìmen的de反应fǎnyìng很hěn不一样bù yíyàng。
Recently our company started using artificial intelligence to write reports, and my colleagues' reactions have been very different.
有人yǒurén担心dānxīn自己zìjǐ的de工作gōngzuò会huì被bèi机器jīqì取代qǔdài，有人yǒurén却què觉得juéde终于zhōngyú可以kěyǐ少shǎo加班jiābān了le。
Some worry their jobs will be replaced by machines, while others feel they can finally work less overtime.
说实话shuō shíhuà，我wǒ两liǎng种zhǒng感觉gǎnjué都dōu有yǒu。
To be honest, I have both feelings.
人工智能réngōng zhìnéng确实quèshí比bǐ我wǒ快kuài：我wǒ写xiě一yí份fèn总结zǒngjié要yào两liǎng个ge小时xiǎoshí，它tā两liǎng分钟fēnzhōng就jiù能néng写完xiěwán。
The AI really is faster than me: it takes me two hours to write a summary, and it can finish one in two minutes.
但是dànshì它tā也yě经常jīngcháng出错chūcuò，而且érqiě错cuò得de特别tèbié自信zìxìn，你nǐ不bù仔细zǐxì检查jiǎnchá就jiù根本gēnběn发现不了fāxiàn bùliǎo。
But it also makes mistakes often, and it gets things wrong with such confidence that you simply cannot notice unless you check carefully.
所以suǒyǐ我wǒ的de工作gōngzuò慢慢mànmàn变biàn了le：以前yǐqián是shì自己zìjǐ写xiě，现在xiànzài是shì判断pànduàn机器jīqì写xiě得de对不对duì bu duì。
So my job has slowly changed: before, I did the writing; now, I judge whether the machine wrote it correctly.
一yí位wèi老lǎo同事tóngshì说shuō，历史lìshǐ上shàng每měi次cì新xīn技术jìshù出现chūxiàn，人们rénmen都dōu害怕hàipà失业shīyè，最后zuìhòu总zǒng会huì出现chūxiàn新xīn的de职业zhíyè。
A senior colleague said that in history, every time a new technology appeared people feared unemployment, yet in the end new professions always emerged.
这zhè句jù话huà也许yěxǔ没错méi cuò，可是kěshì从cóng旧jiù工作gōngzuò到dào新xīn工作gōngzuò的de路lù，往往wǎngwǎng要yào走zǒu很多hěn duō年nián。
Those words may well be true, but the road from the old job to the new one often takes many years to walk.
我wǒ不bù知道zhīdào十shí年nián以后yǐhòu自己zìjǐ会huì做zuò什么shénme，只能zhǐ néng一边yìbiān使用shǐyòng这些zhèxiē工具gōngjù，一边yìbiān继续jìxù学习xuéxí。
I don't know what I will be doing ten years from now; all I can do is keep learning while I use these tools.

Grammar note 语法点

一边…一边…

Doing two actions at the same time: 一边使用工具，一边继续学习。

他一边工作，一边准备考试。— He works while preparing for the exam.
往往

'Often / tend to' — states a general tendency based on experience, not a single event: 从旧工作到新工作的路往往要走很多年。

好机会往往留给有准备的人。— Good opportunities often go to those who are prepared.

Check yourself 小测验

1. Why does the narrator still have to check the AI's reports carefully?
2. What point did the senior colleague make about new technology?
3. How has the narrator's own job changed?
