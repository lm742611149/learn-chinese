数字真有趣

Shùzì zhēn yǒuqù
Numbers Are Fun · 67 words · ~1 min
我wǒ爱ài学习xuéxí汉语Hànyǔ，汉语Hànyǔ的de数字shùzì真zhēn有趣yǒuqù！
I love studying Chinese, and Chinese numbers are really fun!
你nǐ看kàn，一yī，二èr，三sān，都dōu很hěn好hǎo写xiě。
Look — one, two, and three are all easy to write.
十shí和hé一yī是shì十一shíyī，十shí和hé二èr是shì十二shí'èr。
Ten and one make eleven; ten and two make twelve.
我wǒ今年jīnnián三十sānshí岁suì，我wǒ女儿nǚ'ér三sān岁suì。
I'm thirty this year, and my daughter is three.
她tā会huì说shuō很hěn多duō数字shùzì，我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
She can already say lots of numbers, which makes us all very happy.
你nǐ会huì写xiě汉语Hànyǔ的de数字shùzì吗ma？
Can you write numbers in Chinese?

Key words 生词

数字shùzìnumber
真zhēnreally
有趣yǒuqùinteresting; fun
写xiěto write
岁suìyears old
女儿nǚ'érdaughter

Grammar note 语法点

会 + verb

会 means someone can do something because they learned it: 会说 = can say, 会写 = can write.

她会说很多数字。 — She can say lots of numbers.
person + number + 岁

Ages need no verb in Chinese — just put the number and 岁 right after the person: 我三十岁 = I am thirty.

我女儿三岁。 — My daughter is three years old.
sentence + 吗？

Add 吗 to the end of a statement to turn it into a yes/no question — the word order stays the same.

你会写汉语的数字吗？ — Can you write numbers in Chinese?

Check yourself 小测验

1. How old is the narrator this year?
2. Who can say lots of numbers?
3. According to the text, which numbers are easy to write?
