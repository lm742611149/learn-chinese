手机和生活

Shǒujī hé shēnghuó
Phones and Life · 65 words · ~1 min
现在xiànzài,手机shǒujī已经yǐjīng成为chéngwéi我们wǒmen生活shēnghuó中zhōng最zuì重要zhòngyào的de东西dōngxi之一zhī yī。
Nowadays, the phone has become one of the most important things in our lives.
我们wǒmen用yòng手机shǒujī聊天liáotiān、购物gòuwù、看kàn新闻xīnwén,甚至shènzhì工作gōngzuò。
We use our phones to chat, shop, read the news — even to work.
但是dànshì,越来越yuè lái yuè多duō的de人rén发现fāxiàn自己zìjǐ离不开lí bu kāi手机shǒujī了le。
But more and more people find they can't live without their phones.
吃饭chīfàn的时候de shíhou看kàn手机shǒujī,睡觉shuìjiào以前yǐqián也yě要yào看kàn手机shǒujī。
They look at their phones while eating, and again before going to sleep.
我wǒ觉得juéde,我们wǒmen应该yīnggāi放下fàngxià手机shǒujī,多duō陪陪péipei家人jiārén和hé朋友péngyou。
I think we should put down our phones and spend more time with family and friends.
