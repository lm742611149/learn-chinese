HSK 1 · Newbie

我的朋友

Wǒ de péngyou
My Friend · 51 words · ~1 min
我wǒ有yǒu一个yí ge好hǎo朋友péngyou，她tā叫jiào李月Lǐ Yuè。
I have a good friend called Li Yue.
李月Lǐ Yuè是shì医生yīshēng，她tā在zài医院yīyuàn工作gōngzuò。
Li Yue is a doctor; she works at a hospital.
她tā爱ài看书kàn shū，也yě爱ài做菜zuò cài。
She loves reading, and she also loves cooking.
星期天xīngqītiān我们wǒmen一起yìqǐ吃饭chī fàn。
On Sundays we eat together.
她tā做zuò的de菜cài很hěn好吃hǎochī，我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
The food she makes is delicious, and we are both very happy.
我wǒ很hěn喜欢xǐhuan我wǒ的de朋友péngyou。
I really like my friend.

Grammar note 语法点

也 — also

也 goes right before the verb: 她爱看书，也爱做菜 = she loves reading and also loves cooking.

我也是学生。— I am a student too.
叫 — to be called

To give someone's name: person + 叫 + name: 她叫李月 = she is called Li Yue.

你叫什么名字？— What is your name?

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is Li Yue's job?
2. Where does Li Yue work?
3. When do the two friends eat together?
