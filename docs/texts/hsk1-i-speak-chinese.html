我会说汉语

Wǒ huì shuō Hànyǔ
I Can Speak Chinese · 62 words · ~1 min
我wǒ是shì美国人Měiguó rén，我wǒ在zài中国Zhōngguó工作gōngzuò。
I am American, and I work in China.
我wǒ会huì说shuō一点儿yìdiǎnr汉语Hànyǔ。
I can speak a little Chinese.
今天jīntiān中午zhōngwǔ我wǒ去qù饭店fàndiàn吃饭chī fàn。
Today at noon I go to a restaurant to eat.
我wǒ说shuō：“我wǒ吃chī米饭mǐfàn，喝hē茶chá。”
I say, "I'll have rice and tea."
饭店fàndiàn的de人rén听tīng了le很hěn高兴gāoxìng，他tā说shuō：“你nǐ的de汉语Hànyǔ很hěn好hǎo！”
The man at the restaurant hears this and is very pleased; he says, "Your Chinese is very good!"
我wǒ很hěn高兴gāoxìng，我wǒ爱ài学习xuéxí汉语Hànyǔ。
I am very happy — I love studying Chinese.

Grammar note 语法点

会 + verb — learned ability

会 means 'can' for skills you have learned: 我会说汉语 = I can speak Chinese.

她会做菜。— She can cook.
一点儿 — a little

一点儿 goes after the verb to soften the amount: 我会说一点儿汉语 = I can speak a little Chinese.

我喝一点儿水。— I drink a little water.
