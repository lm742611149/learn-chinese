我的房间

Wǒ de fángjiān
My Room · 54 words · ~1 min
我wǒ的de房间fángjiān不bú大dà，很hěn漂亮piàoliang。
My room isn't big, but it's very pretty.
房间fángjiān里lǐ有yǒu一个yí ge桌子zhuōzi和hé一个yí ge椅子yǐzi。
In the room there is a desk and a chair.
桌子zhuōzi上shang有yǒu电脑diànnǎo和hé一些yìxiē书shū。
On the desk there is a computer and some books.
我wǒ喜欢xǐhuan坐zuò在zài椅子yǐzi上shang看书kànshū、写字xiězì。
I like to sit on the chair, reading and writing characters.
我wǒ的de猫māo喜欢xǐhuan在zài我wǒ的de房间fángjiān里lǐ睡觉shuìjiào。
My cat likes to sleep in my room.
我wǒ爱ài我wǒ的de小xiǎo房间fángjiān。
I love my little room.

Grammar note 语法点

Noun + 里 / 上 — location words

Attach 里 (inside) or 上 (on top) after a noun to make a place: 房间里 (in the room), 桌子上 (on the desk). Then use 有 for what is there.

桌子上有电脑。 — There is a computer on the desk.
在 + place + verb

The action's location goes before the verb with 在: 我的猫在房间里睡觉 (my cat sleeps in the room), not at the end like English.

我的猫在我的房间里睡觉。 — My cat sleeps in my room.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is on the desk?
2. Who likes to sleep in the room?
3. How does the narrator describe the room?
