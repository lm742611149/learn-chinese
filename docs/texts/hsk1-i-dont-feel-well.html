我不舒服

Wǒ bù shūfu
I Don't Feel Well · 45 words · ~1 min
今天jīntiān我wǒ不bù舒服shūfu。
Today I don't feel well.
妈妈māma说shuō:“我们wǒmen去qù医院yīyuàn。”
Mom says: "Let's go to the hospital."
医生yīshēng看了看kàn le kàn我wǒ。
The doctor takes a look at me.
医生yīshēng说shuō:“请qǐng多duō喝hē水shuǐ,多duō睡觉shuìjiào。”
The doctor says: "Please drink more water and sleep more."
我wǒ说shuō:“谢谢xièxie医生yīshēng。”
I say: "Thank you, doctor."
明天míngtiān我wǒ想xiǎng去qù学校xuéxiào。
Tomorrow I want to go to school.

Key words 生词

舒服shūfucomfortable; well

Grammar note 语法点

不 + adjective

不 negates verbs and adjectives: 不舒服 means 'not well'.

今天我不舒服。
多 + verb

多 before a verb means 'do more of it'.

请多喝水,多睡觉。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 今天我怎么样?
2. 我们去哪儿?
3. 医生请我做什么?
