HSK 1

买东西

Mǎi dōngxi
Going Shopping · ~1 min read
下午xiàwǔ我wǒ去qù商店shāngdiàn买mǎi东西dōngxi。
In the afternoon I go to the store to buy something.
我wǒ想xiǎng买mǎi一个yí ge杯子bēizi。
I want to buy a cup.
这个zhège杯子bēizi很hěn漂亮piàoliang,多少duōshao钱qián?
This cup is very pretty — how much is it?
商店shāngdiàn的de人rén说shuō:“三十八sānshíbā块kuài。”
The shopkeeper says: "Thirty-eight kuai."
好的hǎo de,我wǒ买mǎi了le。
OK, I'll take it.
我wǒ很hěn喜欢xǐhuan我wǒ的de新xīn杯子bēizi。
I really like my new cup.
