买东西

Mǎi dōngxi
Going Shopping · 45 words · ~1 min
下午xiàwǔ我wǒ去qù商店shāngdiàn买mǎi东西dōngxi。
In the afternoon I go to the store to buy something.
我wǒ想xiǎng买mǎi一个yí ge杯子bēizi。
I want to buy a cup.
这个zhège杯子bēizi很hěn漂亮piàoliang,多少duōshao钱qián?
This cup is very pretty — how much is it?
商店shāngdiàn的de人rén说shuō:“三十八sānshíbā块kuài。”
The shopkeeper says: "Thirty-eight kuai."
好的hǎo de,我wǒ买mǎi了le。
OK, I'll take it.
我wǒ很hěn喜欢xǐhuan我wǒ的de新xīn杯子bēizi。
I really like my new cup.

Key words 生词

商店shāngdiànshop; store
东西dōngxithing; stuff
杯子bēizicup
多少钱duōshao qiánhow much (money)
块kuàiyuan (colloquial)
新xīnnew

Grammar note 语法点

多少钱? — asking the price

多少钱 = how much (money)? In spoken Chinese prices use 块 (kuài) instead of the formal 元: 三十八块.

这个多少钱?——十块。 — How much is this? — Ten kuai.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What does the writer want to buy?
2. How much does it cost?
3. How does the writer feel about the new cup?
