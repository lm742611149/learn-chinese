周末去公园

Zhōumò qù gōngyuán
A Weekend in the Park · 46 words · ~1 min
星期六xīngqīliù天气tiānqì很hěn好hǎo。
On Saturday the weather is great.
我wǒ和hé女朋友nǚpéngyou一起yìqǐ去qù公园gōngyuán。
My girlfriend and I go to the park together.
公园gōngyuán里li人rén很hěn多duō,有人yǒu rén跑步pǎobù,有人yǒu rén唱歌chànggē。
There are lots of people in the park — some are jogging, some are singing.
我们wǒmen坐zuò在zài树shù下xià喝hē茶chá、说话shuōhuà。
We sit under a tree, drinking tea and chatting.
时间shíjiān过guò得de真zhēn快kuài,我们wǒmen玩儿wánr了le一个yí ge下午xiàwǔ。
Time passes so quickly — we spend the whole afternoon having fun.
