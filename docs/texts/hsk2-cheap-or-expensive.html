便宜还是贵

Piányi háishi guì
Cheap or Expensive · 74 words · ~1 min
今天jīntiān上午shàngwǔ,我wǒ去qù商店shāngdiàn买mǎi衣服yīfu。
This morning I went to a store to buy clothes.
我wǒ看见kànjiàn一件yí jiàn白bái衣服yīfu,很hěn漂亮piàoliang,但是dànshì要yào三百sānbǎi块kuài钱qián。
I saw a white top — very pretty, but it cost three hundred kuai.
我wǒ问wèn服务员fúwùyuán:“有yǒu便宜piányi一点儿yìdiǎnr的de吗ma?”
I asked the shop assistant: "Do you have a slightly cheaper one?"
她tā给gěi我wǒ一件yí jiàn黑hēi衣服yīfu,一百五十yìbǎi wǔshí块kuài,比bǐ白bái的de便宜piányi。
She gave me a black top — one hundred fifty kuai, cheaper than the white one.
黑hēi衣服yīfu也yě很hěn漂亮piàoliang,我wǒ就jiù买mǎi了le它tā。
The black top was also very pretty, so I bought it.
我wǒ觉得juéde,便宜piányi的de东西dōngxi也yě可以kěyǐ很hěn漂亮piàoliang。
I think cheap things can be pretty too.

Key words 生词

件jiàn(measure word for clothes)
白báiwhite
黑hēiblack
比bǐthan (comparison)
便宜piányicheap
觉得juédeto feel; think

Grammar note 语法点

A 比 B + adj (comparison)

比 compares two things: A is more ... than B.

一百五十块,比白的便宜。
adj + 一点儿 (a little more...)

一点儿 after an adjective softens it: a bit cheaper, a bit bigger.

有便宜一点儿的吗?

Check yourself 小测验

1. 白衣服多少钱?
2. 我买了什么颜色的衣服?
3. 黑衣服比白衣服怎么样?
