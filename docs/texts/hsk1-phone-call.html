HSK 1 · Newbie

打电话

Dǎ diànhuà
A Phone Call · 81 words · ~1 min
昨天zuótiān下午xiàwǔ，我wǒ很hěn想xiǎng我wǒ的de妈妈māma。
Yesterday afternoon, I missed my mom very much.
我wǒ打电话dǎ diànhuà，说shuō：“喂wèi，妈妈māma，你nǐ好hǎo吗ma？”
I made a phone call and said, "Hello, Mom, how are you?"
妈妈māma说shuō：“我wǒ很hěn好hǎo！你nǐ吃饭chīfàn了le吗ma？”
Mom said, "I'm fine! Have you eaten yet?"
我wǒ说shuō：“吃chī了le。我wǒ在zài学校xuéxiào学习xuéxí汉语Hànyǔ，很hěn高兴gāoxìng。”
I said, "I have. I'm studying Chinese at school, and I'm very happy."
妈妈māma说shuō：“好hǎo，星期天xīngqītiān回家huíjiā吃饭chīfàn，好hǎo吗ma？”
Mom said, "Good. Come home for a meal on Sunday, okay?"
我wǒ说shuō：“好hǎo！妈妈māma，再见zàijiàn！”
I said, "Okay! Bye, Mom!"

Grammar note 语法点

喂 — answering the phone

喂 (wèi) starts a phone call, like English "hello?". It is only used on the phone, not for greeting people in person.

喂，妈妈，你好吗？ — Hello, Mom, how are you?
……，好吗？ — asking "okay?"

Add 好吗 after a suggestion to gently ask for agreement: 星期天回家吃饭，好吗？

星期天回家吃饭，好吗？ — Come home for a meal on Sunday, okay?

Check yourself 小测验

1. Why did the narrator make the phone call?
2. What did Mom ask the narrator?
3. What will the narrator do on Sunday?
