今天的天气

Jīntiān de tiānqì
Today's Weather · 41 words · ~1 min
今天jīntiān天气tiānqì很hěn好hǎo,不bù冷lěng也yě不bú热rè。
The weather is great today — not cold and not hot.
昨天zuótiān下雨xià yǔ,今天jīntiān不bú下xià了le。
It rained yesterday, but today it has stopped.
我wǒ和hé妈妈māma出去chūqù走一走zǒu yi zǒu。
My mom and I go out for a walk.
外面wàimiàn人rén很hěn多duō,大家dàjiā都dōu在zài玩儿wánr。
There are lots of people outside, all having fun.
我wǒ爱ài今天jīntiān的de天气tiānqì!
I love today's weather!

Key words 生词

天气tiānqìweather
冷lěngcold
热rèhot
下雨xià yǔto rain
出去chūqùto go out
外面wàimiànoutside

Grammar note 语法点

不 A 也不 B — neither A nor B

A tidy pattern for two negatives: 不冷也不热 (neither cold nor hot), 不大也不小 (neither big nor small).

今天不冷也不热。 — Today is neither cold nor hot.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is the weather like today?
2. What was the weather yesterday?
3. Who goes out for a walk with the writer?
