我爱运动

Wǒ ài yùndòng
I Love Sports · 39 words · ~1 min
我wǒ很hěn喜欢xǐhuan运动yùndòng。
I really like sports.
每个měi ge星期六xīngqīliù,我wǒ都dōu去qù打篮球dǎ lánqiú。
Every Saturday I go play basketball.
我们wǒmen从cóng九点jiǔ diǎn玩儿wánr到dào十一点shíyī diǎn。
We play from nine to eleven.
打完dǎ wán球qiú,大家dàjiā一起yìqǐ喝hē可乐kělè,吃chī西瓜xīguā。
After the game, everyone drinks cola and eats watermelon together.
运动yùndòng让ràng我wǒ的de身体shēntǐ很hěn好hǎo。
Sports keep me very healthy.

从……到……cóng... dào...from... to...
大家dàjiāeveryone

身体shēntǐbody; health

Grammar note 语法点

从 A 到 B — from A to B

Works for both time and place: 从九点到十一点 (from 9 to 11), 从北京到上海 (from Beijing to Shanghai).

我们从九点玩儿到十一点。 — We play from nine to eleven.

Check yourself 小测验

1. When does the writer play basketball?
2. They play from nine to...?
3. What do they have after the game?
