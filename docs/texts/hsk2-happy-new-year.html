新年快乐

Xīnnián kuàilè
Happy New Year · 101 words · ~2 min
新年xīnnián要yào到dào了le，大家dàjiā都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
The New Year is almost here, and everyone is very happy.
每měi年nián新年xīnnián，我wǒ都dōu回家huíjiā看kàn爸爸bàba妈妈māma。
Every New Year, I go home to visit my mom and dad.
今年jīnnián我wǒ给gěi他们tāmen买mǎi了le茶chá和hé衣服yīfu，还有háiyǒu一些yìxiē水果shuǐguǒ。
This year I bought them tea and clothes, and also some fruit.
妈妈māma做zuò了le很多hěn duō好吃hǎochī的de菜cài，我们wǒmen都dōu吃chī得de很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
Mom made lots of delicious dishes, and we all ate happily.
爸爸bàba对duì我wǒ说shuō：“祝zhù你nǐ新年xīnnián快乐kuàilè，工作gōngzuò好hǎo，身体shēntǐ好hǎo！”
Dad said to me: "Happy New Year — may your work go well and your health be good!"
我wǒ说shuō：“也yě祝zhù你们nǐmen新年xīnnián快乐kuàilè！希望xīwàng你们nǐmen每měi天tiān都dōu高兴gāoxìng！”
I said: "Happy New Year to you too! I hope you're happy every single day!"
新年xīnnián的de时候shíhou，和hé爸爸bàba妈妈māma在zài一起yìqǐ，我wǒ觉得juéde最zuì快乐kuàilè。
At New Year, being together with my mom and dad is when I feel happiest.

Key words 生词

新年xīnniánNew Year
快乐kuàilèhappy
祝zhùto wish (someone something)
希望xīwàngto hope
身体shēntǐhealth; body
水果shuǐguǒfruit

Grammar note 语法点

要 … 了

要…了 says something is about to happen soon, e.g. 火车要开了 'the train is about to leave'.

新年要到了。 — The New Year is almost here.
每 … 都 …

每 (měi) 'every' is almost always paired with 都 (dōu) before the verb, e.g. 我每天都喝茶.

每年新年，我都回家看爸爸妈妈。 — Every New Year, I go home to visit my mom and dad.
Verb + 得 + adjective

得 (de) after a verb introduces how the action is done, e.g. 他说得很好 'he speaks very well'.

我们都吃得很高兴。 — We all ate happily.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What does the narrator do every New Year?
2. What did he buy for his parents this year?
3. What did Dad wish him?
