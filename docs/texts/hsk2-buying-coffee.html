买咖啡

Mǎi kāfēi
Buying Coffee · 53 words · ~1 min
早上zǎoshang我wǒ去qù咖啡店kāfēidiàn买mǎi咖啡kāfēi。
In the morning I go to the coffee shop to buy coffee.
服务员fúwùyuán问wèn我wǒ:“您nín要yào热rè的de还是háishi冰bīng的de?”
The server asks me: "Would you like it hot or iced?"
我wǒ说shuō:“我wǒ要yào一杯yì bēi热rè咖啡kāfēi,不要bú yào糖táng。”
I say: "I'd like a hot coffee, no sugar."
咖啡kāfēi三十sānshí块kuài钱qián,有点儿yǒudiǎnr贵guì。
The coffee is thirty kuai — a little expensive.
但是dànshì很hěn好喝hǎohē,我wǒ每天měi tiān都dōu想xiǎng喝hē。
But it's delicious — I want to drink it every day.
