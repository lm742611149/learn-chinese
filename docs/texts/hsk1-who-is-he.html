HSK 1 · Newbie

他是谁

Tā shì shéi
Who Is He? · 61 words · ~1 min
你nǐ看kàn，这个zhège人rén是shì谁shéi？
Look, who is this person?
他tā是shì我wǒ的de朋友péngyou，他tā是shì汉语Hànyǔ老师lǎoshī。
He is my friend, and he is a Chinese teacher.
他tā在zài学校xuéxiào工作gōngzuò，他tā的de学生xuésheng很hěn多duō。
He works at a school and has many students.
他tā有yǒu一yí个gè女儿nǚ'ér，女儿nǚ'ér五wǔ岁suì。
He has a daughter, and she is five years old.
他tā的de女儿nǚ'ér很hěn小xiǎo，很hěn爱ài看kàn书shū和hé电视diànshì。
His daughter is little — she loves books and TV.
他们tāmen都dōu是shì我wǒ的de好hǎo朋友péngyou。
They are both my good friends.

Grammar note 语法点

…是谁？

To ask who someone is, put 谁 (who) right where the answer would be — the word order does not change.

这个人是谁？ — Who is this person?
的 for possession

的 links a person to what belongs to them, including family and friends.

他是我的朋友。 — He is my friend.
都 (all / both)

都 goes before the verb to say the whole group does or is something.

他们都是我的好朋友。 — They are both my good friends.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What is the friend's job?
2. How old is his daughter?
3. What does the daughter love?
