骑自行车

Qí zìxíngchē
Riding a Bike · 144 words · ~2 min
我wǒ家jiā离lí公司gōngsī不bú太tài远yuǎn，但是dànshì每天měi tiān坐zuò公共汽车gōnggòng qìchē上班shàngbān，车chē上shang人rén很hěn多duō。
My home isn't too far from my company, but I take the bus to work every day, and it's always crowded.
上个月shàng gè yuè，同事tóngshì对duì我wǒ说shuō：“你nǐ为什么wèishénme不bù骑qí自行车zìxíngchē上班shàngbān呢ne？又yòu方便fāngbiàn又yòu能néng锻炼duànliàn身体shēntǐ。”
Last month a colleague said to me: "Why don't you ride a bike to work? It's convenient and it's good exercise."
我wǒ觉得juéde他tā说shuō得de对duì，就jiù买mǎi了le一yí辆liàng新xīn自行车zìxíngchē。
I thought he was right, so I bought a new bicycle.
第一天dì yī tiān骑车qíchē的de时候shíhou，我wǒ的de腿tuǐ很hěn疼téng，骑qí得de也yě很hěn慢màn。
The first day I rode, my legs were sore and I rode very slowly.
可是kěshì我wǒ每天měi tiān都dōu骑qí，一个yí gè星期xīngqī以后yǐhòu，我wǒ骑qí得de越来越yuè lái yuè快kuài了le。
But I rode every day, and after a week I was riding faster and faster.
每天měi tiān早上zǎoshang，我wǒ一边yìbiān骑车qíchē，一边yìbiān看kàn街道jiēdào旁边pángbiān的de树shù和hé花huā，觉得juéde很hěn舒服shūfu。
Every morning I look at the trees and flowers along the street while I ride, and it feels wonderful.
现在xiànzài我wǒ已经yǐjīng习惯xíguàn了le骑qí自行车zìxíngchē上班shàngbān，身体shēntǐ也yě比bǐ以前yǐqián好hǎo多duō了le。
Now I'm used to biking to work, and my health is much better than before.
骑qí自行车zìxíngchē对duì环境huánjìng也yě好hǎo，我wǒ打算dǎsuàn以后yǐhòu一直yìzhí骑qí下去xiàqu。
Riding a bike is also good for the environment, and I plan to keep riding from now on.

Grammar note 语法点

又……又……

又 A 又 B links two qualities or benefits of the same thing: 'both A and B'. For example, 这个菜又便宜又好吃.

骑自行车上班又方便又能锻炼身体。 — Riding a bike to work is both convenient and good exercise.
一边……一边……

一边 A 一边 B describes two actions happening at the same time, e.g. 一边吃饭一边看电视 'eat while watching TV'.

我一边骑车，一边看街道旁边的树和花。 — I look at the trees and flowers along the street while I ride.
越来越 + adjective

越来越 before an adjective means something keeps increasing: 'more and more'. For example, 天气越来越热.

一个星期以后，我骑得越来越快了。 — After a week, I was riding faster and faster.

Check yourself 小测验

1. Who suggested riding a bike to work?
2. What happened on the first day of riding?
3. What does the narrator plan to do in the future?
