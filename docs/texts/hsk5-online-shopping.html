网上购物

Wǎngshàng gòuwù
Online Shopping · 59 words · ~1 min
随着suízhe网络wǎngluò的de发展fāzhǎn,网上wǎngshàng购物gòuwù变得biàn de越来越yuè lái yuè普遍pǔbiàn。
With the development of the internet, online shopping has become more and more common.
人们rénmen不用búyòng出门chūmén,就jiù能néng买到mǎidào世界shìjiè各地gèdì的de商品shāngpǐn。
Without leaving home, people can buy products from all over the world.
网购wǎnggòu虽然suīrán方便fāngbiàn,但dàn也yě存在cúnzài一些yìxiē问题wèntí。
Although online shopping is convenient, it also has some problems.
比如bǐrú,有的yǒude商品shāngpǐn质量zhìliàng不好bù hǎo,和hé图片túpiàn完全wánquán不一样bù yíyàng。
For example, some products are of poor quality and look completely different from the pictures.
因此yīncǐ,消费者xiāofèizhě在zài购物gòuwù时shí应该yīnggāi保持bǎochí冷静lěngjìng,不要búyào盲目mángmù消费xiāofèi。
Therefore, consumers should stay calm when shopping and avoid spending blindly.
