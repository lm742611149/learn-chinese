去朋友家做客

Qù péngyou jiā zuòkè
Visiting a Friend · 62 words · ~1 min
星期六xīngqīliù下午xiàwǔ,我wǒ去qù朋友péngyou家jiā做客zuòkè。
On Saturday afternoon I go to my friend's home as a guest.
我wǒ买mǎi了le一些yìxiē苹果píngguǒ和hé西瓜xīguā送给sònggěi他tā。
I bought some apples and a watermelon to give him.
朋友péngyou的de妈妈māma做zuò了le很多hěn duō好吃hǎochī的de菜cài。
My friend's mom cooked a lot of delicious dishes.
我们wǒmen一起yìqǐ吃饭chīfàn,说shuō了le很多hěn duō话huà。
We ate together and talked a lot.
他tā家jiā的de小猫xiǎomāo很hěn漂亮piàoliang,我wǒ很hěn喜欢xǐhuan它tā。
The kitten at his home is very pretty; I like it a lot.
晚上wǎnshang九点jiǔ diǎn我wǒ回家huí jiā,今天jīntiān我wǒ玩wán得de非常fēicháng高兴gāoxìng。
At nine in the evening I went home; today I had a really great time.

Key words 生词

做客zuòkèto visit as a guest
一些yìxiēsome
西瓜xīguāwatermelon
好吃hǎochītasty
一起yìqǐtogether
非常fēichángvery; extremely

Grammar note 语法点

V + 得 + 状态 (degree complement)

得 after a verb links it to a description of how the action goes.

今天我玩得非常高兴。
V + 了 (completed action)

了 right after a verb marks the action as completed.

我买了一些苹果和西瓜送给他。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我星期六下午去哪儿?
2. 我买了什么送给朋友?
3. 谁做了很多好吃的菜?
