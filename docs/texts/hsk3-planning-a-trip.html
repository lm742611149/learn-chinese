打算去旅行

Dǎsuàn qù lǚxíng
Planning a Trip · 138 words · ~2 min
夏天xiàtiān快kuài到dào了le，我wǒ和hé妻子qīzi打算dǎsuàn出去chūqu旅行lǚxíng一yí次cì。
Summer is almost here, and my wife and I are planning to take a trip.
我wǒ想xiǎng去qù南方nánfāng的de城市chéngshì看看kànkan，可是kěshì妻子qīzi更gèng想xiǎng去qù爬山páshān。
I want to visit a city in the south, but my wife would rather go mountain climbing.
后来hòulái我们wǒmen决定juédìng，先xiān去qù南方nánfāng的de城市chéngshì玩wán三sān天tiān，然后ránhòu再zài去qù爬山páshān。
In the end we decided to first spend three days in the southern city, and then go mountain climbing.
我wǒ在zài网上wǎngshàng看kàn了le很hěn多duō照片zhàopiàn，那个nàge城市chéngshì又yòu干净gānjìng又yòu漂亮piàoliang，街道jiēdào旁边pángbiān都dōu是shì树shù。
I looked at many photos online; the city is clean and beautiful, with trees all along the streets.
妻子qīzi说shuō：“我们wǒmen要yào早zǎo点儿diǎnr买mǎi飞机票fēijī piào，最近zuìjìn坐zuò飞机fēijī的de人rén越来越yuè lái yuè多duō。”
My wife said: "We should buy the plane tickets early — more and more people are flying these days."
买mǎi好hǎo票piào以后yǐhòu，我wǒ把bǎ地图dìtú和hé照相机zhàoxiàngjī都dōu放fàng进jìn了le行李箱xínglixiāng。
After buying the tickets, I put the map and the camera into the suitcase.
如果rúguǒ爬山páshān那天nà tiān下雨xiàyǔ，我们wǒmen就jiù在zài城市chéngshì里li多duō玩wán一yì天tiān。
If it rains on the day of the climb, we'll just spend one more day in the city.
还hái有yǒu两liǎng个gè星期xīngqī我们wǒmen就要jiùyào去qù旅行lǚxíng了le，我wǒ和hé妻子qīzi都dōu非常fēicháng高兴gāoxìng。
In just two weeks we'll be off on our trip, and my wife and I are both really excited.

Key words 生词

打算dǎsuànto plan; to intend
旅行lǚxíngto travel
决定juédìngto decide
爬山páshānto climb mountains
照相机zhàoxiàngjīcamera
行李箱xínglixiāngsuitcase

Grammar note 语法点

如果……，就……

如果 introduces a condition and 就 introduces the result: 'if ..., then ...'. For example, 如果明天下雨，我们就不去.

如果爬山那天下雨，我们就在城市里多玩一天。 — If it rains on the day of the climb, we'll spend one more day in the city.
就要……了

就要…了 says something is about to happen soon, often with a time word before it, e.g. 火车就要开了 'the train is about to leave'.

还有两个星期我们就要去旅行了。 — In just two weeks we'll be going on our trip.
把 + object + verb + 进/好

In a 把 sentence, a direction or result word like 进 or 好 follows the verb to show where the object ends up, e.g. 把书放进包里.

我把地图和照相机都放进了行李箱。 — I put the map and the camera into the suitcase.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What did the couple decide to do first?
2. Why does the wife want to buy plane tickets early?
3. What will they do if it rains on the hiking day?
