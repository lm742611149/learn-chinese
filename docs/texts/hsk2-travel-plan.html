旅游计划

Lǚyóu jìhuà
Travel Plan · 73 words · ~1 min
下个月xià gè yuè,我wǒ和hé妻子qīzi想xiǎng去qù北京Běijīng旅游lǚyóu。
Next month my wife and I want to travel to Beijing.
这zhè是shì我们wǒmen的de旅游lǚyóu计划jìhuà:我们wǒmen准备zhǔnbèi玩wán一个yí gè星期xīngqī。
This is our travel plan: we intend to spend a week having fun.
我wǒ在zài手机shǒujī上shàng买mǎi了le飞机票fēijī piào,不太bú tài贵guì。
I bought the plane tickets on my phone; they were not too expensive.
我们wǒmen还hái找zhǎo了le一个yí gè宾馆bīnguǎn,离lí火车站huǒchēzhàn很hěn近jìn。
We also found a hotel that is very close to the train station.
妻子qīzi说shuō,北京Běijīng有yǒu很多hěn duō好吃hǎochī的de东西dōngxi,她tā都dōu想xiǎng吃chī。
My wife says Beijing has lots of tasty food, and she wants to try it all.
我wǒ希望xīwàng天气tiānqì晴qíng,我们wǒmen能néng玩wán得de很hěn快乐kuàilè。
I hope the weather is sunny so we can have a very happy time.

Key words 生词

计划jìhuàplan
旅游lǚyóuto travel
准备zhǔnbèito plan; prepare
宾馆bīnguǎnhotel
离lífrom (distance)
希望xīwàngto hope

Grammar note 语法点

A 离 B + 近/远 (distance)

离 states the distance between A and B, followed by 近 (near) or 远 (far).

宾馆离火车站很近。
希望 + clause

希望 introduces a whole sentence expressing what you hope for.

我希望天气晴,我们能玩得很快乐。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我们想去哪儿旅游?
2. 我们准备玩多长时间?
3. 宾馆离哪儿很近?
