三口人吃饭

Sān kǒu rén chīfàn
A Family of Three Eats Together · 53 words · ~1 min
我wǒ家jiā有yǒu三sān口kǒu人rén:爸爸bàba、妈妈māma和hé我wǒ。
There are three people in my family: Dad, Mom, and me.
中午zhōngwǔ我们wǒmen在zài家jiā吃饭chīfàn。
At noon we eat at home.
妈妈māma做zuò了le很多hěn duō菜cài和hé米饭mǐfàn。
Mom made a lot of dishes and rice.
爸爸bàba说shuō:“这些zhèxiē菜cài很hěn好吃hǎochī!”
Dad says: "These dishes are delicious!"
我wǒ吃chī了le很多hěn duō米饭mǐfàn,喝hē了le一杯yì bēi水shuǐ。
I ate a lot of rice and drank a cup of water.
我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn高兴gāoxìng。
We are all very happy.

Key words 生词

口kǒu(measure: family members)
中午zhōngwǔnoon
菜càidish; food
米饭mǐfàncooked rice
好吃hǎochītasty (food)
都dōuall

Grammar note 语法点

家 + 有 + number + 口 + 人

口 is the measure word for counting family members.

我家有三口人。
都

都 means 'all/both' and goes before the verb or adjective.

我们都很高兴。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我家有几口人?
2. 谁做了菜?
3. 中午我们在哪儿吃饭?
