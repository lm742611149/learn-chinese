HSK 1

我的一天

Wǒ de yì tiān
My Day · ~1 min read
我wǒ叫jiào大明Dàmíng。
My name is Daming.
我wǒ七点qī diǎn起床qǐchuáng。
I get up at seven o'clock.
我wǒ喝hē茶chá,不bù喝hē咖啡kāfēi。
I drink tea, not coffee.
中午zhōngwǔ我wǒ和hé朋友péngyou吃饭chīfàn。
At noon I eat with my friends.
下午xiàwǔ我wǒ在zài家jiā学习xuéxí。
In the afternoon I study at home.
晚上wǎnshang我wǒ看kàn书shū,十一点shíyī diǎn睡觉shuìjiào。
In the evening I read, and at eleven I go to bed.
