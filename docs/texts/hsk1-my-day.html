HSK 1 · Newbie

我的一天

Wǒ de yì tiān
My Day · 36 words · ~1 min
我wǒ叫jiào大明Dàmíng。
My name is Daming.
我wǒ七点qī diǎn起床qǐchuáng。
I get up at seven o'clock.
我wǒ喝hē茶chá,不bù喝hē咖啡kāfēi。
I drink tea, not coffee.
中午zhōngwǔ我wǒ和hé朋友péngyou吃饭chīfàn。
At noon I eat with my friends.
下午xiàwǔ我wǒ在zài家jiā学习xuéxí。
In the afternoon I study at home.
晚上wǎnshang我wǒ看kàn书shū,十一点shíyī diǎn睡觉shuìjiào。
In the evening I read, and at eleven I go to bed.

Key words 生词

起床qǐchuángto get up
喝hēto drink
中午zhōngwǔnoon
朋友péngyoufriend
学习xuéxíto study
睡觉shuìjiàoto sleep

Grammar note 语法点

Number + 点 — telling the time

点 marks the hour. Put the time BEFORE the verb: 我七点起床 (I get up at seven). Never say 我起床七点.

我十一点睡觉。 — I go to bed at eleven.

Check yourself 小测验

1. When does Daming get up?
2. What does he drink?
3. What does he do in the evening?
