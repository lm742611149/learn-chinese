医院挂号

Yīyuàn guàhào
Hospital Registration · 99 words · ~2 min
昨天zuótiān晚上wǎnshang我wǒ有点儿yǒudiǎnr发烧fāshāo,头tóu也yě很hěn疼téng。
Last night I had a slight fever, and my head hurt a lot too.
今天jīntiān早上zǎoshang,我wǒ和hé同事tóngshì一起yìqǐ去qù医院yīyuàn看病kànbìng。
This morning, I went to the hospital with a colleague to see a doctor.
医院yīyuàn里lǐ人rén真zhēn多duō,我们wǒmen先xiān在zài一楼yī lóu挂号guàhào。
The hospital was really crowded; we first registered on the first floor.
挂号guàhào的de时候shíhou,阿姨āyí问wèn我wǒ:“你nǐ哪里nǎlǐ不bù舒服shūfu?”
While registering, the lady asked me: "Where do you feel unwell?"
我wǒ说shuō:“我wǒ发烧fāshāo了le,可能kěnéng是shì感冒gǎnmào。”
I said: "I have a fever; it's probably a cold."
医生yīshēng给gěi我wǒ做zuò了le检查jiǎnchá,说shuō问题wèntí不bú大dà,让ràng我wǒ多duō喝水hē shuǐ、多duō休息xiūxi。
The doctor examined me and said it was nothing serious, telling me to drink more water and get more rest.
拿ná了le药yào以后yǐhòu,我wǒ就jiù回家huí jiā睡觉shuìjiào了le,现在xiànzài觉得juéde好hǎo多duō了le。
After picking up the medicine, I went home to sleep, and now I feel much better.

Key words 生词

挂号guàhàoto register (at hospital)
发烧fāshāoto have a fever
感冒gǎnmàoto catch a cold
检查jiǎncháexamination; to examine
舒服shūfucomfortable; well
同事tóngshìcolleague

Grammar note 语法点

adj + 多了

Placed after an adjective, 多了 means 'much more (adj)' — used for a big change compared with before.

现在觉得好多了。
多 + verb

Before a verb, 多 means 'do more of' the action, common in advice.

让我多喝水、多休息。

2. 我和谁一起去医院?
