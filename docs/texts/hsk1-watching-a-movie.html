看电影

Kàn diànyǐng
Watching a Movie · 58 words · ~1 min
今天jīntiān是shì星期六xīngqīliù，我wǒ不bù工作gōngzuò。
Today is Saturday, and I don't have to work.
下午xiàwǔ，我wǒ和hé朋友péngyou去qù看kàn电影diànyǐng。
In the afternoon, my friend and I went to see a movie.
电影diànyǐng很hěn好看hǎokàn，我们wǒmen都dōu很hěn喜欢xǐhuan。
The movie was really good, and we both loved it.
看kàn电影diànyǐng的de时候shíhou，我们wǒmen喝hē茶chá、吃chī苹果píngguǒ。
While watching the movie, we drank tea and ate apples.
电影diànyǐng里lǐ的de人rén说shuō汉语Hànyǔ，我wǒ能néng听tīng一点儿yìdiǎnr！
The people in the movie spoke Chinese, and I could catch a little of it!
明天míngtiān我wǒ想xiǎng再zài看kàn一个yí ge电影diànyǐng。
Tomorrow I want to watch another movie.

Key words 生词

电影diànyǐngmovie
朋友péngyoufriend
好看hǎokàngood (to watch); enjoyable

Grammar note 语法点

……的时候 — when / while …

Put 的时候 after an action to say "while doing it": 看电影的时候 (while watching the movie).

看电影的时候，我们喝茶。 — While watching the movie, we drank tea.
能 + verb — can, be able to

能 before a verb expresses ability: 我能听一点儿 (I can catch a little).

我能听一点儿汉语。 — I can understand a little spoken Chinese.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What day is it in the story?
2. What did they eat while watching the movie?
3. What language did the people in the movie speak?
