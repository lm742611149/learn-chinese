银行开户

Yínháng kāihù
Opening a Bank Account · 103 words · ~2 min
我wǒ刚gāng来lái北京Běijīng工作gōngzuò,今天jīntiān上午shàngwǔ去qù银行yínháng开户kāihù。
I just came to Beijing for work, and this morning I went to the bank to open an account.
银行yínháng里lǐ人rén很hěn多duō,我wǒ拿ná了le号hào以后yǐhòu,坐zuò着zhe等děng了le半bàn个gè小时xiǎoshí。
There were a lot of people in the bank; after taking a number, I sat waiting for half an hour.
工作人员gōngzuò rényuán问wèn我wǒ:“您nín带dài护照hùzhào了le吗ma?”
The staff member asked me: "Did you bring your passport?"
我wǒ说shuō:“带dài了le,这zhè是shì我wǒ的de护照hùzhào和hé手机shǒujī号码hàomǎ。”
I said: "Yes, here are my passport and my phone number."
她tā让ràng我wǒ写xiě名字míngzi,还hái让ràng我wǒ想xiǎng一个yí gè密码mìmǎ。
She had me write my name and also asked me to think of a password.
十五shíwǔ分钟fēnzhōng以后yǐhòu,我wǒ就jiù拿到nádào了le新xīn的de银行卡yínhángkǎ。
Fifteen minutes later, I got my new bank card.
有yǒu了le银行卡yínhángkǎ,我wǒ就jiù可以kěyǐ用yòng手机shǒujī花钱huā qián买mǎi东西dōngxi了le,真zhēn方便fāngbiàn!
With a bank card, I can now spend money with my phone to buy things — so convenient!

护照hùzhàopassport
密码mìmǎpassword
银行卡yínhángkǎbank card

Grammar note 语法点

……以后,就……

'After ..., (then) ...' — 以后 follows a time or action; 就 signals that the next thing happens right away.

十五分钟以后,我就拿到了新的银行卡。
让 + sb + verb

让 means 'to have / ask someone to do something', introducing the person before the action.

她让我写名字,还让我想一个密码。

Check yourself 小测验

1. 我今天上午去哪儿了?
2. 我在银行等了多长时间?
3. 工作人员让我想什么?
