HSK 1 · Newbie

这是什么

Zhè shì shénme
What Is This? · 61 words · ~1 min
你nǐ看kàn，这zhè是shì什么shénme？
Look, what is this?
这zhè是shì我wǒ的de杯子bēizi，我wǒ很hěn爱ài喝hē茶chá。
This is my cup — I really love drinking tea.
那nà是shì什么shénme？那nà是shì我wǒ的de书shū。
What is that? That is my book.
我wǒ的de书shū是shì汉语Hànyǔ书shū，我wǒ在zài学习xuéxí汉语Hànyǔ。
My book is a Chinese book — I am studying Chinese.
我wǒ的de桌子zhuōzi很hěn小xiǎo，我wǒ的de电脑diànnǎo很hěn大dà。
My desk is small, but my computer is big.
你nǐ呢ne？你nǐ有yǒu什么shénme东西dōngxi？
How about you? What things do you have?

Grammar note 语法点

这 / 那 + 是 + noun

Use 这 (this) and 那 (that) with 是 (to be) to point things out and say what they are.

这是我的杯子。 — This is my cup.
什么 stays in place

In Chinese, the question word 什么 (what) sits exactly where the answer would go — no word-order change is needed.

这是什么？ — What is this?
的 for possession

Put 的 after a person to show that something belongs to them, like adding apostrophe-s in English.

那是我的书。 — That is my book.

Check yourself 小测验

1. What does the speaker love to drink?
2. What kind of book does the speaker have?
3. What does the speaker say is big?
